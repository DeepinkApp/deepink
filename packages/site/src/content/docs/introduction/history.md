---
title: Notes history
---

Each note have an editing history. Deepink take snapshots of note content periodically when you edit the note, so you can restore specific note edition anytime.

That feature is especially useful to track dynamic of your ideas and processes.

## History control

Some notes may contain a sensitive content and a note snapshots may compromise the privacy in that cases.

You can manually delete specific note snapshots, or disable the history recording at all for specific notes or the whole vault.
