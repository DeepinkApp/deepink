# How to Choose a Car

Choosing the right car depends on your needs and budget.

### Things to Consider

- Price
- Fuel economy
- Safety
- Size
- Maintenance costs

### Popular Types

1. Sedan
2. SUV
3. Hatchback
4. Truck

### Tips

- **Test drive the car** before buying.
- Check the vehicle history.
- Compare different models online.
- *Avoid cars with hidden damage.*

## Car Buying Checklist

- [x] Set a budget
- [x] Research car models
- [ ] Test drive the car
- [ ] Inspect the vehicle history
- [ ] Compare insurance prices

> A reliable used car is often better than an expensive new one.
